What is ISHRAE

The Indian Society of Heating, Refrigerating and Air Conditioning Engineers (ISHRAE), was founded in 1981 in New Delhi, by a group of eminent HVAC&R professionals. ISHRAE today has over thousands of professionals as members, and Student members. ISHRAE is represented by more than 40 Chapters and sub Chapters spread all over India, and is headquartered in New Delhi.
